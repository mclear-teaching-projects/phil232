Week 12 - Leibniz on Matter & Idealism

Date Mon 02 April 2018 Tags Leibniz / matter / idealism

This week we’ll finish our discussion of Locke on knowledge and turn to some of Leibniz’s arguments against the substantiality of matter, and ultimately for the idealist claim that all that really exists are minds.
