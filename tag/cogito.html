Week 2 - Descartes’s Doubts

Date Mon 16 January 2017 Tags method / descartes / science / skepticism / cogito

This week we look at Descartes’s ambitions for constructing a scientific body of knowledge, his criticism of aristotelian empiricism, and his skeptical challenge. We then focus our discussion on Descartes’s famous “cogito” argument in response to the skeptical challenges presented in the First Meditation.
