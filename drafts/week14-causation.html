Week 14 - Hume on Causation & Identity

Date Mon 17 April 2017 Tags hume / mind / causation / necessity / identity / induction

This week we’ll discuss Hume’s famous arguments concerning necessity and personal identity.

Class 20 (4/18)

  • Enquiry, chs. 5-7
  • Notes on Hume on causation
  • In-class notes

Class 21 (4/20)

  • Hume, Section 6 & Appendix to the Treatise on identity
  • (Optional): Enquiry, ch. 12
  • Notes on Identity
  • In-class notes

Reading Questions

  • What is two negative arguments does Hume give against our possession of casual knowledge?
  • What positive characterization does Hume give of causation?
  • What is “custom” on Hume’s view?
  • What does Hume think personal identity consists in?
