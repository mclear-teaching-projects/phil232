Week 7 - Bodies and Causation

Date Mon 19 February 2018 Tags descartes / dualism / causation / review / midterm

This week we finish our discussion of Descartes’s Meditations. Whatever we don’t get to we’ll finish, along with review for the midterm, next Tuesday (2/27). The midterm is on Thursday, March 1st.

You can find the in-class notes here.

Class 9 (2/20)

  • Sixth Meditation, with empahsis on Descartes’s argument for the existence of the external world
  • Correspondence (PDF) with Princess Elisabeth of Bohemia (Stanford Encylopedia entry)

No Class (2/22)

  • Review for Midterm
  • Midterm (Format):
    • Passage recognition
    • Short answer
    • Multiple choice
    • Short essay

Reading Questions

  • If God were deceptive would Descartes’s argument for the external world succeed?
  • In what way(s) do our ideas of material beings resemble those beings?
  • What does Elisabeth object to in Descartes’s dualist account?
